# Abyssal Surge — scenario beats and edited narrator script

**Scenario discipline:** dialogue-first, one pressure turn per stage, and a silent protagonist. This is a game beat sheet—not a standalone webtoon, panel plan, or implementation instruction. Every beat, spoken/text narration line, and cinematic cue resolves to [worldview.md](worldview.md).

## Tension curve

| Stage | Curve (1–10) | Peak / reason |
|---|---|---|
| Cinder Span | entry 3 → spoor 4 → extraction pressure 6 → materialize 5 → relay 7 → boss 8 → turn 9 → reward 6 | The guardian names the Warden’s binding. |
| Veil Citadel | entry 4 → two-node hold 7 → possession 6 → boss 8 → turn 9 → reward 6 | The Tactician denies being a door guard. |
| Echo Throne | entry 5 → throne node 7 → Domain 9 → boss 9 → turn 10 → record 7 | The Sovereign names succession; terminal completion follows. |
