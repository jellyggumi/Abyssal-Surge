# Abyssal Surge — concept

## Player promise

**“명령 하나가 군단 하나를 만들고, 군단 하나가 봉쇄선 하나를 바꾼다.”**

A compact, browser-local RTS-RPG campaign: the silent **Dusk Warden** hunts a rift trace, extracts its shade, materializes a shadow legion, captures a required node, then assaults the stage antagonist. The three-stage story rises from a drowned forge to a listening fortress to the gate above the last remembered sea. Core premise and identity: **AS-WV-001–010**.

## Three-stage spine

| Stage | Story question | Player action truth | Stage turn | Carry-forward reward truth |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Cinder Span / 신더 스팬** | “What is bound in the ash?” | Hunt twice, Extract, Materialize, capture one forge node, then Assault Cinder Warden. | The Warden’s binding is echoed back by the boss. | Choose exactly one of four listed boons; its code effect persists. |
| **Veil Citadel / 베일 시타델** | “Who is the fortress keeping in?” | Carry the first boon, hold two signal nodes, Possess once, then Assault Veil Tactician. | The boss says it does not guard the door. | Choose exactly one of three listed boons for Echo Throne. |
| **Echo Throne / 메아리 왕좌** | “What waits behind the gate?” | Carry both boons, capture the throne node, use one Lord's Domain if needed, then Assault Gate Sovereign. | “Only succession” frames the terminal victory. | Choose one record-only ending reward; campaign completes. |

Stage facts and all public descriptions are held in **AS-WV-011–043**; presentation interpretations are **AS-WV-067–074**.

## Constraints — deliberately preserved

- Exactly three stages: Cinder Span → Veil Citadel → Echo Throne. No fourth stage, epilogue mission, or hidden command.
- The only semantic commands are `hunt`, `extract`, `materialize`, `capture`, `possess`, `domain`, and `assault` (**AS-WV-074**).
- Possession remains a Stage 2 prerequisite; Lord's Domain remains a one-use Stage 3 comeback; Assault remains the final gate in each stage.
- Rewards are earned in the current campaign only. This concept adds no payment, account, multiplayer, or cross-campaign economy claim.
- Optional narrative media must not block text/UI play. Art may use GodTiboImagen provenance only; audio is a separately sourced/validated chatbot-generated import, never presented as GodTiboImagen output.

## Optional storytelling novelty — hypothesis, not a result

**Novelty:** a three-part *containment → prison → succession* reveal, delivered as skippable Korean entry/boss text and optional still-led cinematic cues while the Dusk Warden stays silent. It adds interpretation and recall, not a mechanic.

**Testable hypothesis:** In a moderated, first-play session, after each completed stage, at least **70%** of participants can (a) name that stage’s mandatory objective and (b) select the intended story turn from a three-choice prompt without consulting the UI. Compare the same build with narrative cues shown versus text-suppressed; report `n`, completion state, and both rates. **Status: NOT-RUN; no retention, comprehension, or preference result is claimed.**
