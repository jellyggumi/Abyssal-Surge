**Navigation:** [production contract](../production-contract.md) · [architecture contract](../../engineering/architecture-contract.md) · [retrospective template](../../retrospectives/cycle-1-retrospective.md)

## Entry gate

Enter after Stage 2 passes G2/G3/G5/G7-final/G8, with no open S1 and all evidence paths retained.

## Required work and evidence

1. Establish handset/browser matrix; record measured p95 frame ≤16.7 ms, long frames <0.5%, input ≤100 ms, and 30-minute stable memory.
2. Exercise rollback once and complete release checklist; publish `ops/rollback-runbook.md` and `ops/release-readiness.md`.
3. Complete browser/mobile verification, regression matrix, effect/readability scorecard, and final G1 content audit.
4. Measure G4 with structured immersion median ≥4.0/5, feedback ≤100 ms, and zero unresolved S1/S2 readability complaints.
5. Write final G1/G4/G6 reviews and a validated retrospective using the typed schema.

## Echo Throne continuation

Carry `stage2_reward_id`; unlock only the pre-bounded Lord’s Domain activation; defeat the Gate Sovereign. Do not add APK delivery, accounts, cloud save, co-op, or ranked play to this exit unless their separate contracts and tests exist.

## Exit

G1-final, G4, and G6-final pass only with measured evidence. Cycle closure requires [decision log](../decision-log.md) and [typed retrospective](../../retrospectives/cycle-1-retrospective.md); choose `stage-1-concept-shift` or `stage-2-retune` for the next-cycle entry.
