## Entry gate

Enter only after Stage 1 evidence is attached and director reviews G1/G6-draft/G7. Preserve prior evidence; do not relabel a target as a pass.

## Required work and evidence

1. Define full combat formula, TTK target, unit stats, node curve, possession duration, Domain cooldown/cost, and bounded reversal probability in versioned balance data.
2. Run simulations for all mechanics, combo EV, and matchups; G2 requires 45–55% win rates, TTK ±15%, and no combo >1.3× median EV.
3. Test five archetypes (commander/node control, possession skirmish, slot-efficient swarm, elite formation, comeback recovery); G3 requires three viable and no >50% dominance.
4. Complete five-title novelty frequency matrix and structured impression scoring; G8 requires ≤2 frequency and ≥4/5 impression.
5. Keep revenue disabled unless signed G5 design–PM evidence exists. Any future proposal needs paid/free delta ≤5 pp, reversal ≤30%, and 10–20 session free-path parity.
6. Produce `qa/exploit-register.md`, `qa/playtest-report.md`, `pm/revenue-forecast.md`, runtime balance mirror/simulation logs, and five Stage 2 director reviews.

## Veil Citadel continuation

Carry `stage1_reward_id`; unlock possession; require simultaneous hold of two original signal nodes; defeat the Veil Tactician; offer exactly three exclusive rewards and persist one as `stage2_reward_id`.

## Exit

G2/G3/G5/G7-final/G8 must pass with evidence. A maximum of two FIX loops applies; a third failure needs a director decision-log entry and returns to the appropriate evidence-producing step.
