# Revenue map — fairness-first boundary

**Navigation:** [production contract](../production/production-contract.md) · [reward bands](reward-bands.md) · [G5 handoff](../production/handoffs/stage-2.md)

## Observed fact

The static GitHub Pages Stage 1 client has no approved account, payment, cloud-save, or authoritative multiplayer service. Any paid advantage or client-authoritative reward would be unmeasurable and forgeable in this scope.

## Decision

Stage 1 has **no revenue surface**. Its two stage rewards are earned campaign choices, not purchases. Future candidates are cosmetic palettes, accessibility themes, and optional cloud convenience only after cloud operations are approved; none can alter slots, damage, cooldown, objective progress, or reward availability.

## G5 continuation criteria

Before any monetization decision, establish equal-skill paid/free win-rate delta ≤5 percentage points, reversal probability ≤30% per bounded activation, free-path parity within 10–20 sessions, and a signed design–PM record for every revenue point. Until then G5 is `NOT-RUN`.
