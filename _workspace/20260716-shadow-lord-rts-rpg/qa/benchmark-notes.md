## Observed primary-source patterns

| Comparable | Observed published pattern | Link | Adopted only as an abstract pattern |
|---|---|---|---|
| Warcraft Rumble | deployment economy, map control, leader/boss progression | [Blizzard](https://warcraftrumble.blizzard.com/en-us/) | resource-to-deployment-to-objective cadence |
| Clash Royale | compact loadout and immediate real-time feedback | [Supercell](https://supercell.com/en/games/clashroyale/) | small, legible pre-stage choice |
| Clash of Clans | persistent base/army choices and balance update cadence | [Supercell](https://supercell.com/en/games/clashofclans/) | persistent choice separated from short stage |
| Bad North | compact tactical terrain and consequential stage reward | [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/688420/Bad_North_Jotunn_Edition/) | authored modifier plus replayable consequence |
| Kingdom Two Crowns | build/defend/expand and solo/co-op framing | [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/701160/Kingdom_Two_Crowns/) | defer shared-resource co-op until solo loop is stable |

## Boundary

These are observations, not content sources. QA must reject copied names, writing, art, sound, mechanics expressed as a clone, or unproven novelty claims. Use [worldview](../design/worldview.md) for the original-content audit.
