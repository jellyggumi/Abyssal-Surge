## Observed comparables

The five-title source set is Warcraft Rumble, Clash Royale, Clash of Clans, Bad North: Jotunn Edition, and Kingdom Two Crowns. Their primary-source links and observations are in [QA benchmark notes](../../qa/benchmark-notes.md).

## Decision filter

Keep compact deployment, map-objective pressure, consequential stage reward, and optional later co-op as patterns. Reject third-party lore, cards/decks, monetization, names, art direction, campaign writing, and any claim of novelty without frequency evidence.

## Stage 1 finding

The smallest defensible public beat is not a broad RTS: it is a one-Lord, three-role, five-objective offline vertical slice whose reward alters the next stage. This supports G7 measurement while keeping the IP boundary auditable.
