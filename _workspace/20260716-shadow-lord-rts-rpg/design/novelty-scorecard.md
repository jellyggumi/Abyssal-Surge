## G8 threshold

At least one element appears in no more than 2 of at least 5 surveyed comparable titles and earns QA impression score ≥4/5. This card does **not** assert that threshold has been met.

## Candidate

**Decision:** assess the compound mechanic “defeat → 10-second targetable pool → immediate bounded legion materialization → capture pressure,” rather than claiming its individual ingredients are new.
